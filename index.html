About Us

I'm Prashant Kumar, PhD scholar(PMRF) at IIT Kanpur in Department of Aerospace Engineering. I'm working with Dr. Rajesh Ranjan (ACAL) in the area of Scientific Machine Learning that includes Physics-informed Neural Network of Incompressible and compressible flows for turbomachinery flows.

Traditional methods have difficulty with complex, low Reynolds Number flows in turbine blades, which hinders design optimisation. PINN solves this problem by combining machine learning and physics comprehension. Unlike deep learning models, PINN does not require massive amounts of data and takes flow physics into account. Extending PINN to compressible flows (common in turbines), predicting blade performance (e.g., T106A, VKI-LS89), and optimising designs to reduce separation and wake losses are all components of the research. It includes the prediction of turbine blade performance in transitional flow conditions. "
